This machine learning solution addresses high employee turnover in BPO environments through predictive analytics. By analyzing HR data patterns, it identifies employees at risk of leaving before they resign, enabling proactive intervention and informed retention strategies.

Three-Stage Process

1. Data Collection: Integrates HR records, engagement surveys, team metrics, compensation data, and external market information.

2. ML Processing: Uses Random Forest and XGBoost algorithms with SHAP analysis to recognize patterns, score risk, and pinpoint key factors influencing turnover.

3. Actionable Insights: Delivers targeted retention strategies, focuses interventions, and tracks ROI through executive dashboards.
