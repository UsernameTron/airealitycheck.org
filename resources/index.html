Resources & Tools

This resource is designed to help you better use AI without complex jargon or technical knowledge. Simple, practical, and accessible.

Practical Tools

Image Prompt Modifier + Organizer

A clean, web-based tool that helps you build better image prompts — even if you have no idea what "anamorphic lens" means. Click modifiers, stack up a prompt that actually sounds cinematic, and copy-paste it into your image model of choice.

No film degree. No guessing. Just results.

Access Tool

Video Prompt Optimizer

This one's for the video generation folks. Click your way through camera movements, moods, and scene styles without pretending you're Spielberg. It generates clean, structured prompts using cinematic terms most people don't even know exist.

  • AI video tools like Runway, Pika, etc.
  • People who don't want to Google "Dutch angle" mid-project
Access Tool

Plain English Prompt Builder

Built on Streamlit, this tool lets you speak like a human ("I want a summary of X with examples and stats"), choose your model, click once, and boom — usable prompt, copy-paste ready. It's natural language to optimized engineering without the headache.

For the "I don't want to learn prompt theory, I just want it to work" crowd.

Access Tool

Deep Research Prompt Generator (GPT Store)

This GPT is designed to help you structure deep research prompts — the kind that ask for citations, studies, actual stats, and no BuzzFeed energy. Perfect for content, strategy, or when you want your AI to fake having a PhD.

Access Tool

Advanced Reasoning Prompt Generator (GPT Store)

This one's built for complex thinking. It walks you through creating structured, multi-step prompts that make the AI reason like it has a second brain cell. Ideal for strategy work, comparisons, decisions, or when you're tired of surface-level sludge.
